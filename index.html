About

I am currently the only student in the one year Orthodontic Clinical Preceptorship at Rutgers School of Dental Medicine (RSDM), Department of Orthodontics. I am applying for Orthodontic Residency this cycle. I graduated from Rutgers School of Dental Medicine (RSDM) in 2024, with a Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD), being one of two students in my class to have a, "Distinction in Special Care" for my involvement in the NJLEND Fellowship Program.

I am currently the only Clinical Preceptor at RSDM Department of Orthodontics. In this position, I perform the same duties as the Orthodontic Residents, such as attending to my own patients and their orthodontic needs, diagnosis, treatment planning, banding and bonding, archwire change, and debonding. I have utilized our department's new 3D printing lab to create Essix retainers from 3D prints, scanned using iTero scanners.

I was one of two dental fellows to be admitted into the NJLEND (New Jersey Leadership Education in Neurodevelopmental and Related Disabilities program) Fellowship Program. This program is under the umbrella of the The Boggs Center on Disability and Human Development, Rutgers Robert Wood Johnson Medical School. In this fellowship, we learned weekly about individuals with developmental disabilities, the challenges they face on a daily basis, and the ways in which we can advocate and help them.

I had the privilege of participating in a Mission Trip along with several classmates and faculty at Rutgers School of Dental Medicine to La Romana, Dominican Republic.
